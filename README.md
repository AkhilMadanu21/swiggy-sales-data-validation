# 🍔 Swiggy Sales Data Analysis Project

## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy food delivery data to extract meaningful business insights.
The goal is to perform **data cleaning, validation, and exploratory analysis** to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem (BRD)

The analysis is driven by key business metrics:

* **Total Sales (₹)** – Overall revenue generated
* **Average Rating** – Customer satisfaction level
* **Average Order Value (AOV)** – Revenue per order
* **Total Orders** – Number of orders placed
* **Rating Count** – Total number of customer reviews

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Validation

* Checked and handled missing values
* Removed duplicate records
* Standardized data types
* Created new time-based features (Month, Day, Quarter, Year)
* Categorized food items into **Veg / Non-Veg**

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 📅 Time-Based Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Daily Sales Trend
* Weekly Performance Analysis
* Quarterly Sales Summary

### 🍽 Category Analysis

* Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)
* Revenue by Cuisine

### 🌍 Location-Based Analysis

* State-wise Revenue Distribution
* Top 5 Cities by Sales

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 📈 Sales show consistent growth across months
* 📅 Higher sales observed during weekends
* 🍽 Veg items contribute a major share of total revenue
* 🌍 A few top cities contribute significantly to overall sales
* ⭐ Average rating (~4.3) indicates strong customer satisfaction

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights strong business performance with steady growth and high customer satisfaction.
Key opportunities include focusing on high-performing cities and optimizing product offerings based on demand trends.
